My Journey to the Highest road in the world

The Mighty Umling La - 19,024 ft

I Started my solo journey from my hometown Surat and it got culminated at the Highest motorable road in the world after 8 days and 3000+ kms. Umling La, 19024 ft lies in the Ladakh Region of the Indian Himalayas and it is just 30 kms away from the border between India and Chinese occupied region of Tibet. In my quest to reach the top of the world on my belowed Motorcycle, I experienced probably everything from magnificent scenary to the gigantic mountains, the lack of Oxygen and Motorcycle breakdowns. But in the end everything was worth it when you reach there.
